Privacy Policy

How Chan Digital collects and uses data for the Badminton Go app and website.

Last updated: June 24, 2026

1. Who we are

Chan Digital ("we", "us", "our") operates the Badminton Go Garmin app and the website at chandigital.in/badmintongo/. Contact us at support@chandigital.in.

2. Data collected by the watch app

Badminton Go runs on your Garmin device. On-device, the app stores:

  • Settings — your preferences (game mode, score limits, bottom stat choice) stored locally via Garmin Application.Storage.
  • License state — a claim code and license status flag (free / trial / paid) stored locally. No personal data is included.
  • Session count — a counter for free trial sessions, stored locally on the device.

None of this data is transmitted by the app itself. Activity data (heart rate, duration) is handled by Garmin and governed by Garmin's privacy policy.

3. Data collected on this website

When you purchase or restore a license at chandigital.in/badmintongo/, we collect:

  • Name and email address — used to send your license confirmation and to process refund requests.
  • Claim code — the device-generated code used to bind the license to your watch.
  • Payment information — processed directly by Razorpay (India) or PayPal / Dodo (international). We do not store card numbers or payment credentials.

4. How we use your data

  • To activate and restore your license.
  • To respond to support requests.
  • To process refunds within our 24-hour guarantee period.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Third-party payment processors

Payments are processed by Razorpay (India) and PayPal or Dodo (international). Each processor's own privacy policy governs how payment data is handled. We receive only a transaction confirmation and the claim code you submitted.

6. License data storage

Activated licenses are stored on a Cloudflare Workers KV store. The record contains your claim code, email (hashed), and license status. We do not store full email addresses in the license record.

7. Cookies and analytics

chandigital.in does not use analytics cookies or third-party tracking scripts. No cookie consent banner is shown because no tracking cookies are set.

8. Data retention

Purchase records (name, email, claim code) are retained for up to 3 years to support license restore requests and legal compliance. You may request deletion at any time by emailing support@chandigital.in.

9. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, email support@chandigital.in with "Privacy Request" in the subject line.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app or website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, email support@chandigital.in.
