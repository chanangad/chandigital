FAQ

RallyPoint

Common questions about installation, scoring, purchasing, and getting support.

Which Garmin devices does RallyPoint support?

RallyPoint supports nearly all current Garmin wearables with a Connect IQ app display, including Fenix 7/8 series, Forerunner 255/265/955/965, Venu 2/3, Epix, Instinct 2, Vivoactive 4/5, and more. Check the full compatibility list on the Connect IQ listing.

How does scoring work?

During a game, swipe down (or use the primary button) to add a point to your side. Swipe up (or use the secondary button) to add a point to your opponent. The score updates instantly and the serve indicator moves automatically according to official badminton rules.

What if I score the wrong point?

Press the Back button during a game to undo the last point. RallyPoint keeps a full history for the current game, so you can step back as many times as you need.

Does it support doubles?

Yes. When you start a game you can choose Singles or Doubles. In Doubles mode, the serve indicator tracks both service courts, and player positions rotate according to standard badminton rules.

How many free sessions do I get?

Your first app launch is completely free — no account or payment required. After that, a one-time purchase unlocks the app permanently on your Garmin device.

How much does the full app cost?

RallyPoint is $2.99 (worldwide) or ₹149 (India). It's a one-time purchase — no subscription, no recurring fees.

How do I unlock the full app after my trial ends?

When the trial ends, the watch shows a lock screen with a claim code. Visit chandigital.in/rallypoint/, enter the claim code, complete payment, then return to the watch and select Refresh License from the menu.

I got a new watch. Can I transfer my license?

Yes. Open the app on your new watch, note the new claim code from the lock screen, then visit chandigital.in/rallypoint/, switch to the Restore tab, and enter your new claim code plus the email used at purchase.

The Refresh License step on the watch isn't working.

Make sure your watch is connected to your phone and has an active internet connection via Bluetooth. Choose Check License from the app menu and wait a few seconds. If it still shows as locked, email support with your claim code and purchase email.

Does RallyPoint record a Garmin activity?

Yes. When you end a match and choose Save & Exit, RallyPoint saves the session as a generic activity to your Garmin Connect history, including heart rate data (if your device supports it), duration, and calorie estimates.

What does the bottom stat show, and can I change it?

The bottom of the game screen shows either the current wall-clock time or the elapsed match timer. Go to Settings → Bottom Stat from any menu (pre-game, pause, or game-over) to switch between Clock and Timer.

Can I see my past matches?

Yes. Open the app, go to Game Settings → History to see your last 20 matches with date, result, and game-by-game scores. Tap any match for a detailed breakdown. You can also clear history from that menu.

Can I contact Chan Digital for support?

Yes — email support@chandigital.in. Include your claim code (shown on the watch lock screen) and your purchase email for fastest resolution.
